Our Mission

Real World

LSY provides practical real world examples to the youth of ways to live out the principles of Saint John Baptise De La Salle. It aids the youth in social aspects of their lives. Students from different grades and social groups meet once a week to discuss how their week influenced them and what occurred allowing their peers to gather insight into them and the world around them.
